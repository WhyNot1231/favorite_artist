Pink Floyd


Brief History


Pink Floyd started in London in 1965, by art students, who, in their early shows experimented with lights during their performances. Eventually they got some attention from a record label, and released 5 LPs before their huge hit, The Dark Side of the Moon, in 1973.

After their success with The Dark Side of the Moon, they released 3 more full-length albums, including, in order of releases, Wish You Were Here, Animals, and The Wall, all to great success. Though the band would lose its lead singer, Roger Waters soon after, Pink Floyd have released 4 more albums since, their last and latest in 2014. They have left a lasting impact on popular music.
